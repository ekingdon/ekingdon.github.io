About Allan

The Head of the Elf Technician Team has worked at GNSS for over 40 years, courtesy of a time loop that keeps him stuck in the same position in time, space, and salary. He began his career as a software pirate, sailing the seven seas in search of floppy disks and DAT tapes to plunder. On a lengthy voyage sailing north out of Port Stanley, he realised the error of his ways when he deprived some small children of their Christmas copies of "E.T." for the Atari 2600, and reformed his character.

Today, he also works part time as an expert in the field of post-prandial analysis and soporific equipment testing, in addition to his role at GNSS
