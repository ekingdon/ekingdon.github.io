About Me

I am the right hand Elf to the head honcho, the head cheese, the man himself, the Big Red, the Claus of all Clauses, the one and only, Santa. I grew up in a small village 50 km south of the north pole, the toy town as we called it; this is where it all started. I started as the summer elf wrapping presents in 1802 for Mr Claus, it was my first real job in the toy industry. In my late 30s I became a supervisor in the desining department, a big step for an elf. I designed everything from toy trains to supersonic gumdrop shooters for the governement elf squadrons! My next promotion led me here when Mr. Claus himself mentored me in the sleigh industry. Eventually becoming head mechanic then head elf which I have held for 85 years!

So if you are looking for a great reliable sleigh, trust this old Elf, I know my stuff!
