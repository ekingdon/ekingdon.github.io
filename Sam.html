About Me

Sam joined the team in the winter of 1982 as a reindeer assistant and has quickly refined her reindeer caretaking skills over the years. This resulted in a promotion to the Main Reindeer Caretaker in 1990. Over the years she has won over the hearts of all of Santa's reindeer: Dasher, Dancer, Prancer, Vixen, Comet, Cupid, Donner and Blitzen.

She is always looking to expand her reindeer family and is anticipatingly waiting to meet and love your reindeers!

Qualifications and Achievements

  • Reigning Champion of the Reindeer Caretaking at the North Pole Games
  • Certification of Reindeer Caretaking from the North Pole College
  • Master's Degree in Reindeer Health from the North Pole University
  • Bacholor's Degree in Animal Biology
